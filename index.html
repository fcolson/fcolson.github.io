Custom Game Engine

Watch the engine demo above, or open it on YouTube.

Core Technologies:

  • C++17
  • Lua scripting via LuaBridge
  • GLM for math vectors and matrices
  • SDL2 for windowing, input handling, and audio playback
  • Box2D for physics simulation and raycasting
  • RapidJSON for JSON-based template loading
  • Custom EventBus & ParticleSystem modules
  • AudioDB, ImageDB & TextDB resource managers
